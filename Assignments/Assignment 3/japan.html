Tokyo, Japan

As a kid, I spent a lot of time watching Japanese Anime. So many of them centered around this city that I have always dreamed of going there. The culture and technology that Japan has is just amazing and something I need to see with my own eyes

Tokyo is like the New York of Japan. The lights and the nightlife are just amazing. The food would be slightly more difficult for me to enjoy as I do not like seafood, but I will make it work!

Fun Facts

Here are some fun facts I found about Japan. There are over 6,800 islands in Japan. Trains in Japan are the most punctual in the world. 24 billion chopsticks are used in Japan each year.
